# 📖 About The Project

The **Zomato Analytics Dashboard** is a Power BI Business Intelligence solution designed to analyze revenue performance, customer behavior, delivery operations, restaurant profitability, and order management.

This dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor KPIs, identify trends, evaluate operational efficiency, and make data-driven decisions through interactive visualizations.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

✅ Monitor Revenue & Profit Performance

✅ Analyze Customer Ordering Patterns

✅ Evaluate Delivery Efficiency

✅ Measure Restaurant Profitability

✅ Track Order Status & Customer Satisfaction

✅ Compare City & Regional Performance

✅ Support Strategic Business Decisions
